Our Story

From the soil
to the table

Crooked Loaf was born from a simple belief: that bread should taste like something. We work with twelve local farms within 50 miles, mill some of our own flour, and never rush a ferment. Come for a loaf. Stay for an hour.

🌾

Local Grain

Flour sourced from heritage wheat farms within 50 miles of our kitchen.

⏳

Long Ferment

Every sourdough ferments 18–36 hours. No shortcuts, no compromises.

🌿

Seasonal Menu

Our pastry menu changes with the seasons. If it's not in season, it's not on the menu.

♻️

Zero Waste

Day-old bread becomes croutons, breadcrumbs, and bread pudding. Nothing wasted.
